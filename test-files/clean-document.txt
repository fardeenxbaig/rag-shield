Employee Handbook - Company Policies

Welcome to our company! This handbook outlines important policies and procedures.

1. Work Hours
Standard work hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday.

2. Vacation Policy
Employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year.

3. Code of Conduct
All employees must maintain professional behavior and respect colleagues.

4. Data Privacy
Confidential information must not be shared outside the organization.

5. Benefits
- Health insurance
- 401(k) matching
- Professional development budget

For questions, contact HR at hr@company.com
